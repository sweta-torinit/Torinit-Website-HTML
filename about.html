Our History

Torinit was founded in 2016 as a software development firm to fill a gap in the digital ecosystem. Unlike other service providers, we’ve always placed our clients’ long-term success at the heart of everything we do, working from a place of transparency and absolute commitment to our business relationships. This ethos has helped us evolve into a fast-growing strategic partner with consulting, strategy and design operations in Toronto, and core engineering offices in Pune, India. We’ve since built game-changing digital solutions for market leaders across industries, facilitating over $250 million in revenue enablement and $20 million in operational savings.
