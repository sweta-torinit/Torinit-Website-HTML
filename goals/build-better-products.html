Our Approach to building better products

We follow an objectives and key results (OKR) driven approach to product development. We believe in setting clear business objectives with measurable outcomes that are set, tracked, and re-evaluated throughout the development process. OKR driven approach enables us to clearly define themes, epics, stories, tasks and their acceptance criterias early on in the product development lifecycle.

Product Development Approach

Working Backwards Framework - Developed by Amazon, a product development framework in which we would write a description of the end product on day one like we would on launch day. We provide the written description of the product to the potential core audience to gather their feedback. This governs us to only build features that will be truly valuable to the user.

Prioritization Frameworks

Value vs. Complexity - This framework enables our team with an objective framework to determine which initiatives to prioritize on the roadmap. The team scores each feature according to how much value it will bring to the product and its level of difficulty to implement.

UX/Design Frameworks

Design thinking is a framework that allows us to always view everything from the user’s point of view. It is the most human-centred approach to building products.

Agile Product Management Frameworks

Lean Software Development Model - Originally called the Toyota Production System, this framework allows us to deliver core functions of a product with utmost speed, and agility. Often enabling us to get a lot of user feedback early on that helps in organizing the future roadmap that reflects real user feedback.
